Long-form article sample

The next generation of translation tools should preserve the reading rhythm of the original page. A good bilingual mode should not force every sentence into a noisy block, and a full-page mode should avoid breaking layout or translating code-like terms incorrectly.

For finance and technology content, accuracy depends on keeping tickers, company names, monetary amounts, product names, and abbreviations stable. The translation should sound natural, but it must not rewrite uncertainty into certainty.

Selection translation is useful when the reader only needs to understand one phrase, one sentence, or one quote. The fastest interaction is to enable the feature once and then translate automatically whenever text is selected.
